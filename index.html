About Insect

Insects are the most diverse group of animals on Earth, with millions of species inhabiting
almost every corner of the globe. These incredible creatures are vital to our ecosystem, playing crucial
roles in pollination, decomposition, and food chains.





DRAGONFLIES
Scientific Name : Odonata
Found : Freshwater bodies: lakes, rivers, ponds, and marshes
Fact : incredible flyers: They can fly forward, backward, hover, and even mate in mid-air!





BUTTERFLIES
Scientific Name : Lepidoptera
Found : Butterflies can be found almost anywhere in the world
Fact : Butterflies taste with their feet!





GRASSHOPPER
Scientific Name : Orthoptera
Found : Grasshoppers are most commonly found in lowland tropical forests,semi-arid regions, and grasslands
Fact : Grasshoppers have ears on their bellies





BEETL
Scientific Name : Coleoptera
Found : Beetl are found almost everywhere on Earth,
except for the harshest environments like the polar regions and the open ocean
Fact : Beetl have a harder exoskeleton than any other insect





ERATHWORM
Scientific Name : Lumbricus terrestris
Found : Earthworms are found in soil almost everywhere on Earth
Fact : Earthworms have no lungs,They breathe through their skin





CENTIPEDE
Scientific Name : Chilopoda.
Found : Centipedes are typically found in damp, dark places
Fact : Centipedes have flat bodies to help them squeeze into tight spaces
